When a company points a subdomain like shop.example.com to an external service (e.g. Shopify, Heroku, Vercel), they create a DNS record linking the two. If they later stop using that service but forget to remove the DNS record, the subdomain becomes dangling.

An attacker can then register that same service account and claim the subdomain — serving their own content under your domain. This is a subdomain takeover.

shop.example.com → some-old-app.herokuapp.com → ??? (unclaimed)

This tool follows the full CNAME chain of any subdomain and checks whether the endpoint is unclaimed, using fingerprints for 100+ known services.
